How Can ifs Help?

 

Franchising is a highly regulated industry, which makes it critical for any franchise sales personnel to both understand and follow the regulations governing the franchise sales process. Our customers can rest assured that ifsSolutions possesses the necessary experience and knowledge to sell their franchises professionally. In particular, we completely understand how federal and state laws impact the franchise sales process, why disclosure documents and marketing materials must reflect regulatory requirements, and what requirements must met in order to offer franchises in registration and non-registration states.

 


In addition to selling your franchise offering, IFS will also make itself available to perform the following:

 

Strategic planning for franchise development and growth initiatives

 

Business consultation on the continued enhancement of your franchise agreement and Uniform Offering Circular (U.F.O.C.)

 

Business consultation on the registration of your franchise agreement in all registration states

Business consultation on the design and implementation of franchisee recruitment and marketing strategies

 

Business consultation on the development and implementation of your franchise company’s organizational structure

 

Business consultation on franchisee’s and franchisor’s web site design and structure

 

Business consultation on the administration of franchisee’s national advertising fund.

 

Business consultation on general franchisee relations and communications

 

Business consultation on developing a franchisee advisory council (F.A.C.)

 

Business consultation on the development and implementation of e-mail (HTML) marketing programs
